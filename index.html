Biography

I am currently a Peng Tsu Ann Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematics, National University of Singapore. I obtained my Ph.D. at the Department of Mathematics at Johns Hopkins University (August 2018 - February 2023). My advisor was Professor Chenyang Xu and my co-advisor was Professor Jingjun Han. My Ph.D. thesis is titled "The minimal log discrepancy and its applications in birational geometry". I received my B.Sc. in Mathematics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2018. You can find my CV here.
